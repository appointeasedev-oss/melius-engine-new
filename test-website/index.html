Beach Activities

Enjoy a variety of fun activities at our beautiful beach. From sunbathing to beach volleyball, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

  • Sunbathing: Relax on our soft, sandy beach.
  • Beach Volleyball: Join a game with friends or family.
  • Beachcombing: Search for seashells and other treasures.
  • Picnicking: Enjoy a meal with a view of the ocean.

Water Sports

Dive into adventure with our exciting water sports. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, we have activities for all skill levels.

  • Surfing: Catch the perfect wave.
  • Snorkeling: Explore the underwater world.
  • Kayaking: Paddle through calm waters.
  • Jet Skiing: Experience the thrill of speed.

Beach Information

  • Location: 123 Beach Road, Paradise Island
  • Hours: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM Daily
  • Facilities: Showers, restrooms, snack bar, and equipment rentals
  • Safety: Lifeguards on duty from 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
